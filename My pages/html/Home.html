Boosters


Everyone 12+, regardless of which COVID-19 vaccine they first received, should get a booster to help protect against severe disease caused by infection with COVID-19. Some people may also get a second booster. Learn more about COVID-19 booster recommendations, or COVID-19 booster recommendations for people who are moderately or severely immunocompromised.
